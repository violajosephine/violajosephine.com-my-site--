G’day mate!
How are ya? My name is Viola and this picture, while being a few years old, sums me up pretty accurately. I love myself some nasi campur after a good surf. Not sure I should be using that word as freely as I am.

I am 21years old and am trying to figure life out. But daaaaaym it aint so easy huh. “I don’t know” is my answer to most questions I ask myself, as of lately, but I do have some pretty interesting thoughts I would argue and I thought I’d share them, in case they might be interesting for someone else as well. However, I am the kid of gal who needs a opinion detox every now and again, because sometimes the amount of “shoulds” I pick up from…errrbody, is a little overwhelming, and so if you feel wrong in any way or anything I write makes you feel stressed out, feel free to put on Bridget Jones and just lax the tired mind.

And yeah. That’s about it. We need less perfect and more just…really cool and complex human who are totally loved and safe, even though it surely doesn’t feel like it sometimes!

Love, Viola
